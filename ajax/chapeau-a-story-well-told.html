CHAPEAU - A STORY WELL TOLD

4 25.05.2015

In our work with clients, we often need to start by guiding them towards the discovery of their own unique story. Sometimes we therefore need to change the perception, that the own story is of no interest or can’t be told in an exciting way. This probably would have been the case for the „Parisian Gentleman“. In short, the „Parisian Gentleman“ is a blog about mens fashion and style, bespoke suits and handmade shoes. So a kind of French blog of the typical Savile Row customer. That’s nothing too exciting. So how could one tell a story and create a viewing experience that leaves a long lasting impression? Well, watch yourself:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YuzxK_U_t0w We couldn’t have done it any better. Therefore, chapeau to DLV BBDO (http://www.dlvbbdo.com).

It’s not about clothes but about style, class and manners. It is about the conviction on how a man should behave, present and carry himself in order to preserve culture, beauty and civilization. Because a world without Gentlemen is a world without Ladies.
